Section 3

What are the rules governing trade into and from the EU for species covered by the Regulations?

Section 3.1
Overview
An overview of the documents required for importing, exporting, and re-exporting listed species into and from the EU.
Section 3.2
What document for what purpose?
Identifying which permit or certificate is required depending on the species, Annex listing, and type of trade.
Section 3.3
What are the rules for the issuance of import permits for specimens of Annex A or B-listed species?
Applying for import permits, completing the required forms correctly, and meeting documentation requirements.
Section 3.4
How are import notifications for specimens of Annex C or D-listed species obtained?
Obtaining import notifications for Annex C and D species, gathering export documents, and submitting forms to customs.
Section 3.5
What documents are required for (re-)export of specimens of species listed in Annex A, B or C?
Obtaining export permits and re-export certificates from the Management Authority before exporting specimens.
Section 3.6
Are there derogations from the normal import and export rules?
Circumstances where import and export rules are less strict, including captive-bred specimens, antiques, and personal effects.
Section 3.7
Trade involving EU dependent and other territories
Understanding how EU wildlife trade rules apply to dependent and overseas territories, and who to contact.
